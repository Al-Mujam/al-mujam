Our Quran & Tajweed Programs
Level 1: Foundations of Quranic Recitation
Prerequisites: Successful completion of our Noorani Qaida Course or equivalent foundational Arabic reading skills. This ensures all students have the necessary foundation to benefit fully from Tajweed instruction.

Focus Areas: Introduction to Arabic letters in Quranic context, basic Tajweed rules, and recitation of short Surahs (Al-Fatiha, Al-Ikhlas, Al-Falaq, An-Nas).

Perfect for: Students who have completed Noorani Qaida and are ready to begin formal Tajweed study and Quranic recitation.

Learning Outcomes: Students will be able to read and recite short verses of the Quran with basic Tajweed rules, understand the importance of proper pronunciation, and feel confident in their initial connection with the sacred text.

Duration: 8-12 weeks with 2 classes per week

Level 2: Intermediate Tajweed Rules
Focus Areas: Advanced Tajweed rules including Noon Sakinah and Tanween, Meem Sakinah, various types of Madd (elongation), and developing fluency in longer passages.

Perfect for: Students who can read Arabic letters but want to improve their Tajweed and develop more beautiful, accurate recitation.

Learning Outcomes: Confident recitation of longer Surahs with correct application of intermediate Tajweed rules, improved flow and rhythm in recitation, and deeper appreciation for the beauty of Quranic Arabic.

Duration: 12-16 weeks with 2 classes per week

Level 3: Advanced Recitation & Ijazah Preparation
Focus Areas: Perfecting recitation through detailed study of advanced Tajweed topics, introduction to different Qira'at (recitation styles), and preparation for obtaining an Ijazah.

Perfect for: Advanced students seeking mastery of Tajweed and those interested in eventually teaching Quranic recitation to others.

Learning Outcomes: Mastery of Tajweed with the ability to recite the entire Quran with the highest level of proficiency, qualification to pursue Ijazah certification, and preparation to become a teacher of Quranic recitation.

Duration: 20-24 weeks with 2-3 classes per week

Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need any prior Arabic knowledge to begin? For our Level 1 Tajweed classes, students must first complete our Noorani Qaida Course or demonstrate equivalent Arabic reading skills. This prerequisite ensures you have the foundational knowledge needed to succeed in Tajweed study. If you are a complete beginner, we recommend starting with our Noorani Qaida Course first.

Are classes taught by male or female instructors? We have both male and female certified instructors available. You may request your preference when enrolling, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.

Can I eventually obtain an Ijazah through your program? Yes, we offer Ijazah preparation for advanced students who demonstrate mastery of Tajweed rules and complete recitation of the Quran. This traditional certification allows you to teach Quranic recitation to others.

What teaching materials and resources do you provide? We use a carefully curated combination of traditional Islamic texts and modern educational resources. All materials are provided, including digital copies for online students and physical books for in-person learners.

How do online classes work technically? Our online classes use professional video conferencing platforms with screen sharing capabilities. Students receive all necessary materials in advance, and classes are interactive with real-time feedback from instructors.
